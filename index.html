01

Overview

PixVally is a photo editing application developed by Adhook Media. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

02

Information We Access

PixVally accesses photos and images stored on your device solely for the purpose of editing. We do not collect, store, or share your photos or any personal data on our servers.

Your photos stay on your device. We never upload or store your personal images.
03

AI-Powered Features

Some features in PixVally use third-party AI services to process images. When you use these features, the selected image is temporarily sent to the service provider's servers for processing only. Images are not stored permanently by these services and are deleted immediately after processing.

04

Permissions

PixVally requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • Storage / Media access — to let you select and save edited photos
  • Internet access — required for AI-powered editing features only

These permissions are used strictly within the app and for no other purpose.

05

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
06

Children's Privacy

PixVally is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.
