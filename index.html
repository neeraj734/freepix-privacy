Privacy Policy for Freepix

Effective date: April 13, 2026

Freepix is developed by Adhook Media. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

Information We Access

Freepix accesses photos and images stored on your device solely for the purpose of editing. We do not collect, store, or share your photos or any personal data.

Third Party Services

Freepix uses ClipDrop API for AI-powered features like background removal and image enhancement. Images processed through these features are sent to ClipDrop servers and are not stored permanently.

Permissions

The app requests access to your device storage and media to allow you to select and save edited photos. These permissions are used only within the app.
